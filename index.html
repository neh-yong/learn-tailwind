Frequently Asked Questions

What is ToDesktop?

ToDesktop lets you convert your existing web application into a native desktop application for Windows, macOS, and Linux without rewriting your codebase.

Which platforms are supported?

Applications built with ToDesktop can be distributed on Windows, macOS, and Linux, allowing you to reach users across all major desktop operating systems.

Do I need Electron experience?

No. ToDesktop handles the Electron configuration and packaging process, allowing you to focus on building your web application.

Can I update my desktop app after publishing?

Yes. ToDesktop supports automatic updates so your users can receive the latest features, bug fixes, and security improvements without manually downloading new versions.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes. You can start with the free plan to test and develop your desktop application, then upgrade to a paid plan when you need advanced features, distribution, and commercial support.
